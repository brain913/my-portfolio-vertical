## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is recruiters and hiring managers evaluating a student portfolio quickly. They need to understand capability, credibility, and fit within a short scan.

### Brand Personality
Editorial, personal, and intentional. Confident but not flashy, with a focus on clarity and proof over decoration.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial portfolio with clear narrative flow and stronger spacing hierarchy. Prioritize scannability and emphasis on contact conversion. Keep the current dark foundation but reduce monotony in repeated section structures.

### Design Principles
- Lead with clarity: make contact pathway and strongest proof obvious within the first viewport.
- Use rhythm in spacing: tight grouping for related items, generous separation between sections.
- Reduce repetition: avoid identical section/card treatments across the entire page.
- Show evidence early: surface strongest projects and outcomes without requiring extra clicks.
- Keep motion purposeful: animation should support reading flow, never compete with content.
